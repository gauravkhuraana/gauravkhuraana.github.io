## Testing Philosophy
- **Testing is exploration** - Not just following scripts
- **Testing is about learning** - Understanding the system deeply
- **Testing provides information** - Helping stakeholders make decisions
- **Testing is a skill** - That improves with practice and reflection

## Tester Mindset
- **Curiosity** - Always asking "what if?"
- **Critical thinking** - Questioning assumptions
- **Empathy** - Understanding user perspectives
- **Attention to detail** - Noticing what others miss
- **Communication** - Sharing findings effectively

## Your Role as a Tester
- **Be the advocate for quality** - Champion good practices
- **Be the voice of the user** - Represent their needs
- **Be a collaborator** - Work with the team, not against it
- **Be a learner** - Continuously improve your skills
