  • Schedule daily tasks: Use your calendar as a commitment tool, not just a reminder

Follow this simple framework every day:

Morning Setupâ€‹

  1. Identify three key things you want to accomplish today
  2. Determine what resources you need to monitor and complete these tasks
  3. Prioritize them based on urgency and importance

Common Signsâ€‹

  • Feeling tired or fatigued
  • Unproductive despite being busy
  • Getting worked up or stressed

Immediate Solutionsâ€‹

  1. Take a walk - Fresh air and movement reset your mind
  2. Give yourself a break - Step away from your current task
  3. Make a cup of tea - A small ritual to create mental space
  4. Step away from your desk - Change your physical environment
  5. Abandon the task temporarily - Sometimes the best solution is to stop
  6. Distract yourself briefly - Allow your mind to reset
  7. Return when focused - Come back when something isn't working and you have clarity
