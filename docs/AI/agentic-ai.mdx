## What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that possess agency - the ability to autonomously make decisions, take actions, and adapt their behavior to achieve specific objectives. Unlike traditional AI that responds to prompts, agentic AI systems can can independently plan, execute, and adapt their actions to achieve complex goals.
